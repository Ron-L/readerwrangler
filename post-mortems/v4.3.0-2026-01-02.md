## üìö Lessons Learned

### 1. **Browser Tab Titles Matter for UX**
- Version numbers in tab titles waste valuable space
- Shortened title "ReaderWrangler - Organize Your Amazon Kindle Library" is more informative
- Version info moved to in-app header where it's visible when needed

### 2. **Book Count Placement**
- Moving from header to filters row groups related controls together
- Book count is contextual to filtering (shows filtered count)
- Header stays clean and focused on branding
